Sam Coffey

Personal Statement

Sam Coffey is a 4th Year Computer Science student at DIT and soon to be an Associate Software Engineer at MasterCard. Prior to beginning this degree course, Sam worked for seven years in a professional office environment and gained experience in building client and business relationships, negotiation, sales, teamwork, IT, accounts, organisation and administration.

During Sam’s internship at MasterCard he was given exposure to an array of technologies and programming languages, with a focus on technologies relating to web services. He also gained experience with Agile software development and was assigned the role of Scrum Master for the MasterCard Reward System Web Services team.

As a Computer Science student at DIT, Sam has developed numerous skills across a wide range of areas, with particular emphasis on software development. He has always had an interest in computers and technology, is enthusiastic, hard-working and has a strong desire to continue his professional development in the area of Computer Science.
